Kelavarapalli Dam

The Krishnagiri Dam, also known as Krishnagiri Reservoir Project (KRP) Dam, is located near Krishnagiri town in Tamil Nadu, India. Built across the Thenpennai River, this dam was constructed in 1958 to support irrigation and water supply in the region. Surrounded by scenic hills and lush greenery, the Krishnagiri Dam is not only a vital source of water for agriculture but also a popular picnic spot for locals and tourists.
